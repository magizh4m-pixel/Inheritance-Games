The Inheritance Games

The Plot

Avery Grambs is an ordinary girl trying to survive life. But everything changes when billionaire Tobias Hawthorne dies and leaves her his entire fortune. To claim it, she must live inside Hawthorne House — a sprawling mansion filled with coded messages, secret passageways, and dangerous riddles. The Hawthorne heirs are brilliant, rich, and suspicious… especially when none of them know why she was chosen. But the deeper Avery goes, the clearer it becomes: This inheritance was never just money. It was a puzzle.
